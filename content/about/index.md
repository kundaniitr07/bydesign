## Hi, I'm Kundan Kumar

I'm a product thinker and systems architect who believes that the best products aren't just well-designed — they're well-structured.

I work at the intersection of product design and system architecture, where user needs meet technical constraints and business goals. My work involves taking complex, messy problems and turning them into elegant, scalable solutions that teams can build and users can love.

## What I Do

I design **product systems** — not just features, but the architecture that connects them. This means thinking about how a product grows over time, how teams collaborate to build it, and how users experience it as a coherent whole.

My work spans across supply chain technology, B2B marketplaces, and enterprise platforms, where I've designed systems that serve thousands of users and process millions of transactions.

## My Philosophy

**Simplicity is a feature.** The best products feel effortless, but that effortlessness is the result of deep thinking and deliberate design choices.

**Systems over features.** Individual features are easy. The hard part is designing a system where every feature makes every other feature better.

**Impact over output.** I'd rather ship one thing that changes a metric by 50% than ten things that each move it by 1%.

## Beyond Work

When I'm not designing product systems, you'll find me exploring new ideas in AI, writing about product thinking, and mentoring aspiring product designers.

I'm always open to conversations about product architecture, systems design, or the future of B2B technology. Feel free to [reach out](/contact/).
